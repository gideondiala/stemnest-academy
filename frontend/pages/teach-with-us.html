🔍 What is a DBS Check?

DBS stands for the Disclosure and Barring Service — a UK government body that helps employers make safer recruitment decisions.

A DBS check reveals any criminal convictions, cautions, warnings, or reprimands on a person's record. For roles working with children and young people, this is a legal safeguarding requirement in the United Kingdom.

At StemNest Academy:

  • All UK-based tutors working with under-18s are required to hold a valid Enhanced DBS certificate.
  • We guide you through the application process — it is straightforward and typically takes 2–4 weeks.
  • StemNest Academy covers the cost of the DBS check once you are accepted onto the platform.
  • Tutors based outside the UK will be required to provide an equivalent background check from their country.

For more information, visit the official DBS website: gov.uk/dbs
