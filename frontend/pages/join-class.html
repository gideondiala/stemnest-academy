📋 Class Rules & Tips for a Great Session
💻
Use a laptop or desktop
Phones make it hard to see and share work.
🔇
Find a quiet spot
Reduce background noise so your tutor can hear you clearly.
📷
Camera on, please
Seeing each other makes learning much more effective.
⏰
Join 2–3 minutes early
Test your mic and camera before the session starts.
📓
Have a notebook ready
Write down key points — it helps you remember.
🙋
Ask questions freely
There are no silly questions. Your tutor is here to help.
🌐
Good internet connection
Use Wi-Fi if possible for the best video quality.
😊
Have fun!
Learning is exciting — enjoy every moment of your class.
